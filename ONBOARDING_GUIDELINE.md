✅ Driver Onboarding Flow: Overview
🎯 Goal:
Register, verify, and activate a driver in under 15 minutes — while ensuring quality, safety, and legality.

🧩 Step-by-Step Driver Onboarding Flow
1. Welcome Screen
Purpose: Introduce your platform's value for drivers

Elements:

“Earn more with fair rates”

“Set your own schedule”

“Get paid fast”

CTA: "Get Started"

2. Account Creation
Email + password or phone number with OTP

Optional: Google/Apple login

Agree to Terms of Service and Privacy Policy

3. Personal Information
Full name

Date of birth

Mobile number

Home city / coverage area

Referral code (optional)

4. Driver’s License & ID Verification
Upload front/back of:

Government-issued photo ID

Valid driver’s license

Optional: Real-time selfie for match verification

(Use a service like Stripe Identity, Jumio, or Onfido for automated checks)

5. Vehicle Information
Vehicle make, model, year

License plate number

Vehicle type (sedan, SUV, wheelchair accessible, etc.)

Upload:

Vehicle registration

Proof of insurance

6. Background Check Consent
(Required in most U.S. jurisdictions)

Ask for SSN or equivalent, address history

Integrate with a service like Checkr, GoodHire, or local equivalent

Show a message: “Approval may take up to 24 hours”

7. Bank Details for Payout
Input or connect bank account via Stripe Connect, Plaid, or PayPal

Explain payout terms (e.g., instant payouts or weekly direct deposit)

8. Set Availability
Let driver choose:

Days of the week

Hours per day

Radius/coverage zone

9. Review and Submit
Summary of submitted data

Allow user to edit anything

CTA: “Submit for Review”

10. Status & Onboarding Completion
Show real-time onboarding status:

✅ Profile complete

⏳ Background check pending

🚫 Action needed (e.g., missing insurance)

Provide helpful resources while they wait:

“How payouts work”

“Maximizing your income”

“Driver ratings explained”

✅ Once Approved:
Unlock access to driver dashboard

Show short tutorial: how to accept rides, cancel, contact riders, etc.

Let them go online to accept jobs

🧠 Optional Enhancements
Gamified progress bar (e.g., 70% complete)

Chat or help center during onboarding

Document resubmission support

In-app notifications for approvals, missing docs, etc.

🔧 Tools to Use
Need	Tools/Services
ID & document scanning	Stripe Identity, Onfido, Jumio
Background checks	Checkr, GoodHire
Payments & payouts	Stripe Connect, PayPal, Plaid
File upload	AWS S3, Firebase Storage
Notifications	Expo Push Notifications, OneSignal
